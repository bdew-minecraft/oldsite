Network Vulnerability in BDLib for Minecraft 1.7-1.12

 Minecraft Modding  Comments Off on Network Vulnerability in BDLib for Minecraft 1.7-1.12
Jul 312023
 

Older versions of BDLib for Minecraft 1.7 – 1.12 contain a known security vulnerability.

You can read more about the vulnerability here – https://blog.mmpa.info/posts/bleeding-pipe

I’m releasing updated builds for 1.7.10 and 1.10.2, a version for 1.12 will be available soon(tm).

Updated: A version for 1.12.2 was added as well.

In general I do not recommend playing those old versions on public open servers, even with those fixed versions – there’s still many other known vulnerable mods from other authors out there.

This does not affect single player.

If you do want to play multiplayer I recommend installing one of those 2 mods, though I can’t guarantee they completely fix the issue – https://github.com/dogboy21/serializationisbad/releases or https://modrinth.com/mod/pipeblocker

I’m alive! Also 1.12. And other News.

 Minecraft Modding  Comments Off on I’m alive! Also 1.12. And other News.
Jul 262017
 

As some of you may have noticed I haven’t been around much lately. I have taken a bit of a break from minecraft modding due to burn out and general busyness… But i’m back now!

One of the reasons for my burn-out was trying to support multiple MC versions at once, and as a result I’ve decided to focus my efforts on 1.12 now, even though the versions for 1.10/11.2 weren’t really finished.

That being said, last week I’ve released updates with some fixes for Gendustry for MC 1.10.2 and 1.11.2 and I also plan to release 1 final build for Advanced Generators and Pressure Pipes for 1.11.2 soon™. For those still running or making packs for older versions – those should be stable enough, even if they aren’t 100% finished.

As for 1.12 – BDLib, Gendustry and JEI Bees have alpha builds for MC 1.12 now up on CurseForge and my Jenkins:

  • BDLib – CurseForge / Jenkins
  • Gendustry – CurseForge / Jenkins
  • JEI Bees – CurseForge / Jenkins

Advanced Generators and Pressure Pipes will also be updated soon™.

Simple Covers, will be also updated later, and the rewrite to MCMP2 finished.

AE2 Stuff… currently undecided. Might make an update to 1.10.2 to work with the stable release of AE2 (that came out a few days ago after no updates for half a year…). It also might or might not get ported to 1.12 and Applied Llamagistics (unofficial fork of AE2).

Mod updates – 07/12/2015

 Advanced Generators, Gendustry, Pressure Pipes  Comments Off on Mod updates – 07/12/2015
Dec 072015
 

I’ve been somewhat busy over the last few months with RL crap and other projects, so not much in terms of new stuff.

Mostly a bunch of small bugfixes and tweaks, stay tuned for more stuff as my schedule goes back to something saner!

Gendustry v1.6.3

  • Added support for item output into Mekanism pipes (using Eject Cover)
  • Added client-side option for bee particles frequency
  • Imprinter will now mark output as analyzed if using a full template or input is analyzed
  • Updated to forestry 4.2.0 which is now the minimum supported version

Advanced Generators v0.9.19

  • Added support for item output into Mekanism pipes
  • Added rubber wood as carbon source
  • Added IC2 EV Emitter
  • Fixed fuel and energy remaining above capacity after removing tanks and/or capacitor modules
  • Fixed energy duplication with IC2 Emitters due to ENet being stupid
  • Fixed Mekanism biofuel in syngas produser
  • Fixed some fuels from other mods not working

Pressure Pipes v1.2.5

  • Fixed issues with tanks larger than 2^31 mB (using custom configs)
