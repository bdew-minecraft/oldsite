Minecraft 1.7.2 – state of things

 Gendustry, Minecraft Modding, NEI Addons  Comments Off on Minecraft 1.7.2 – state of things
May 112014
 

With other mods starting to move to 1.7.2 rapidly, i’m planning to switch most of my development as well. Here’s a short list of my mods, where they stand and what i have planned.

  • NEI Addons
    • 1.10.4 – Current released version.
    • 1.10.5 – Will include Ex Nihilo support, will probably be the last version for 1.6.4 unless bug fixes are needed.
    • 1.11.x – Minecraft 1.7.2 version, alpha is available. ExNihilo support will be ported from 1.6.4 soon ™.
  • Gendustry
    • 1.1.1 Hotfix 1 – Current released version.
    • 1.1.2 – Will include custom bees system, will probably be the last version for 1.6.4 unless bug fixes are needed.
    • 1.2.x – Minecraft 1.7.2 version, alpha is available. Mostly up to date with 1.1.x changes.
  • Deep Core Mining
    • Development is currently stalled. No 1.7.2 version available. Will get back to working on it at some point. I hope. Maybe.
  • EU/MFR integration
    • Dead for all intents and purposes. RIP.

NEI Addons alpha for MC 1.7.2

 NEI Addons  Comments Off on NEI Addons alpha for MC 1.7.2
May 012014
 

Downloads on jenkins

Requirements

  • Minecraft 1.7.2
  • Forge >= 10.12.1.1060
  • Not Enough Items >= 1.7.2-1.0.0
  • Code Chicken Core >= 1.7.2-1.0.0

Supported Mods

  • Forestry >= 2.4.0.0-711 (download from here)
  • Extra Bees (not updated yet)
  • Crafting tables addon
    • BuildCraft – Auto Workbench – 5.0.x and 6.0.x supported
    • Equivalent Exchange 3  – Minium Stone – No working 1.7.2 builds.
    • Andrew2448’s addon for MMMPSs – In-Place Assemvler – Mod status unclear. No 1.7.2 version.
    • Tinkers’ Construct – Crafting Stations

Support removed

  • Misc Peripherals – wasn’t updated since 1.5.x
  • Applied Energistics  – will not be updated to 1.7.2

Known Issues

  • Forestry worktable [?] button doesn’t work in client-only mode – Fixed in build #2
  • All item groups are disabled as NEI lacks support for now
