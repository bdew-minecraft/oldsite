High Pressure Pipes

 Add comments
 

“So basically non-wireless tesseracts?” – Immibis, 2014

  • Connect outputs and inputs
  • Fluids are instantly transported, throughput is unlimited
  • Multiple fluids can be transported at the same time
  • Can be placed in any shape, loops (that don’t include other blocks) and intersections will not cause problems
  • Loops that connect the output from a block or valve back to itself will explode!
  • If ForgeMultipart is installed – can be covered by covers and pass through hollow covers
